Fun at
First Play

Beach Bum was born in 2015 out of genuine passion for gaming and technology. The global gaming company is focused on classic hit card and board games played everywhere. In October 2021, Beach Bum was acquired by hyper-casual gaming leader Voodoo, opening the door to countless new opportunities. With famous hit titles like Lord of the Board, Spades Royale, Gin Rummy Stars, Domino Go and Rummy Rush, Beach Bum entertains millions of players worldwide.
